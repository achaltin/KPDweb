About us

On November 16, 1904, five women from Lewis Institute in Chicago, Illinois decided to establish a sorority based on the bond of friendship that had grown between them during their years together in High School and in college.

Now, over 100 years later, with a newly earned sorority house, Kappa Phi Delta still continues to create and foster bonds of long lasting and powerful friendships between women from a variety of backgrounds, of different races and religions, and from different parts of the world.
